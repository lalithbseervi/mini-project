General Questions

What services do you offer? +

We offer a comprehensive range of web development services including Static Websites, Dynamic Websites, Full-Stack Development with Hosting Support, E-commerce Solutions, Custom Web Applications, Website Maintenance, SEO Optimization, and Content Management Systems.

How long does it take to build a website? +

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project:

  • Static Website: 1-2 weeks
  • Dynamic Website: 4-8 weeks
  • Full-Stack with Hosting: 8-12 weeks

We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

What is your development process? +

Our development process includes:

  1. Discovery & Planning: Understanding your requirements and goals
  2. Design: Creating wireframes and mockups
  3. Development: Building your website
  4. Testing: Ensuring everything works perfectly
  5. Launch: Deploying your website
  6. Support: Ongoing maintenance and updates

Technical Questions

Will my website be functional on all screen sizes? +

Absolutely! All our websites are built with responsive design, ensuring they work perfectly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. Mobile-friendliness is a standard feature in all our packages.

Can I update the website myself? +

Yes! For dynamic websites and full-stack solutions, we provide an easy-to-use admin panel where you can update content, add pages, upload images, and manage your website. We also provide training to help you get started.

Do you provide hosting services? +

Hosting is included in our Full-Stack + Hosting package for one year. For other packages, we can recommend reliable hosting providers or help you set up hosting separately. We also offer ongoing hosting management services.

Will my website be SEO-friendly? +

Yes! We implement basic SEO best practices in all our websites, including proper meta tags, semantic HTML, fast loading times, and mobile optimization. For advanced SEO services, we offer dedicated SEO optimization packages.

Support & Maintenance

Do you provide support after the website is launched? +

Yes! All our packages include post-launch support:

  • Static Website: 3 months support
  • Dynamic Website: 6 months support
  • Full-Stack + Hosting: 24/7 support

After the initial period, we offer extended support and maintenance packages.

What if something breaks on my website? +

During the support period, we'll fix any bugs or issues at no extra cost. For issues after the support period, we offer maintenance packages starting at ₹5,000/month. For urgent fixes, we provide emergency support services.

Can you redesign my existing website? +

Absolutely! We offer website redesign services to modernize and improve your existing website. We can work with your current content and data, or help you create fresh content. Contact us for a redesign consultation.
